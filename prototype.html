Mosquito Larvae Detection Prototype

AI-Powered Detection and Dosage Calculation System for Vector Control

Project Overview

Our mosquito larvae detection prototype represents a breakthrough in automated vector control technology. This innovative system combines computer vision, machine learning, and precise dosage calculation algorithms to revolutionize mosquito population management.

The prototype addresses critical challenges in public health by providing accurate, real-time detection of mosquito larvae in various water bodies and automatically calculating the optimal treatment dosage required for effective control.
