Future Vision for Vector Control

This conceptual app design showcases how our mosquito larvae detection technology could be implemented in a user-friendly mobile interface. The concept demonstrates the potential user experience for field workers, researchers, and public health officials.

While still in the research and development phase, this design concept illustrates how AI-powered detection could be made accessible through intuitive mobile technology, streamlining vector control operations worldwide.
